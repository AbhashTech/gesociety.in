About Gokhale Education Society

  • Established in 1918. Ninety Five Years of Quality Service.
  • Three Zones - Nashik, Mumbai & Thane with 17 Centres and 121 Institutions.
  • 1,25,000 Pupiles, 4000 Staff, Current Budget Rs. 160 Crores.
  • All Colleges and Schools are ISO 9001:2000 Certified.
  • The Society is known for Students' Discipline, Academic Excellence, Professional Competence and Innovative Programmes with a track record of Students and Staff achievements.
  • Society's Colleges have permanent affiliation and recognition for Ph.D. programmes.
  • Internationally known for making Higher and Technical Education Entrepreneurial Innovative and Rewarding through this Nasik Centre (Certified at 44th International Conference- University of Twente, Netherland- Holland.)
